Atlas of the Sound of the Body

From sound to sign. You translate a body sound into a visible form, through the methods below.

What to do

Choose at least one sound from the set below — or more.

Translate it with the core methods: a drawing A or/and a digital artifact B.

Then, if you want, add any of the additional methods.

Format is free — tools, paper, scale, materials.

The soundsS01 — S17
Core methodsone or both
A

B
